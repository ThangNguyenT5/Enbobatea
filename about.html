EN BOBA Tea

2020

EN BOBA Tea is a relatively new bubble tea shop that opened its doors in 2020 in Arizona. The shop is a haven for bubble tea enthusiasts and those seeking to satisfy their sweet cravings with authentic Taiwanese desserts.
